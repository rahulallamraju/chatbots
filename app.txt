This chatbot analyzes the financial data of the 3 companies 
Microsoft, Tesla and Apple
Then you can ask it questions about the revenue, net income, cash flow, 
assets and liabilities.
It can show you the change over the years in the different categories. 
It looks out for keywords such as net income or cash flow and shows you the data
of all companies within these categories. 
When you ask for financial health, it shows equity and assets standings of the 
companies. 
You can also compare the companies and it will show the 3 companies in greatest to 
least in all the categories.
